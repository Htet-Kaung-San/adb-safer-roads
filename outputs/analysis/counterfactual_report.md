# Counterfactual Policy Simulation
*Based on Nilsson Power Model × VOSL × VMT proxy. These are relative indices for ranking interventions — not calibrated absolute predictions.*

## Thailand
### Scenario A — Full Safe System Adoption
- **401 segments** corrected to Safe System threshold
- **$1153.5M USD/year** economic value (VOSL × Nilsson × VMT proxy)
- **$11535M USD** in economic value over 10 years

### Scenario B — Urban Primary/Trunk Reform Only (Single Policy Decision)
- **200 segments** — achievable via one national regulation
- **$868.5M USD/year** economic value
- **$8685M USD** in economic value over 10 years
- No capital expenditure required — speed limit change only
